Before you write in

Guidelines & common questions

Every product page includes a size chart in centimetres alongside model measurements. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for our rugby knits and crewnecks, which are cut for a relaxed fit. You can also build a Fit Profile for a personal recommendation before you buy.

Unworn, unwashed items with tags attached can be returned within 14 days of delivery for store credit or a refund. Start a return by emailing your order number to hello@vestura.com and we'll send a prepaid label.

Orders are packed and shipped within 2 business days. Domestic delivery across India typically takes 3–6 business days depending on the destination. You'll get a tracking link by email as soon as your order leaves the studio.

Not yet — we currently ship within India only. We're working on international shipping and will announce it on our socials once it's live.

If your order hasn't shipped yet, email us with your order number as soon as possible and we'll do our best to amend or cancel it. Once it's shipped, it'll need to go through the standard return process instead.

Every box, mailer and swing tag is made from compostable, plant-based material embedded with seeds — plant it after unboxing and it breaks down into the soil.
